The Couple

Ajla & Seth

It started at the Cat's Pajamas Tiki bar in Downtown Austin, when Ajla leaned over and asked Seth if he liked Lana Del Rey, the song playing, not knowing he had no idea what was on. He said yes anyway.

Not long after, Seth was making the thirty-minute drive out to Pflugerville Texas and back, again and again, just to spend a little more time with her, before finally asking her to be his girlfriend.

These days you'll find them getting cappuccinos, watching terrible reality television, and counting down to the next beach trip — which is exactly why, when it came time to celebrate, they wanted to bring the palm trees and the sand to everyone's November.

Getting There

The Courthouse Gardens are tucked within the landmark Santa Barbara Courthouse, right in the heart of downtown. Street parking and public garages are within easy walking distance, and the venue is just a few blocks from State Street.

Where to Stay

Hotel Recommendations

Santa Barbara is a small, walkable city — wherever you stay, you'll be close to everything. We spent a few days there in April and have some favorites to share, but feel free to find your own perfect spot.

Closest to the Wedding

#1

Casa Jardín

A serene boutique hotel with a garden-lined setting in the heart of downtown — minimalistic design, on-site parking, and EV chargers, just minutes from the courthouse.

~0.2 mi to ceremony  ·  ~0.3 mi to reception

#2

The Upham Hotel

California's oldest continuously operating hotel (est. 1871) — a Victorian gem with sweeping verandas, lush gardens, and a complimentary wine happy hour each evening.

~0.4 mi to ceremony  ·  ~0.3 mi to reception

#3

Cheshire Cat Inn

An elegant B&B with gracious English hospitality and a touch of whimsy — rooms, cottages, and private suites spread across a downtown property just minutes from the best of Santa Barbara.

~0.6 mi to venues  ·  Private cottages available

#4

Drift Santa Barbara

A 45-room design hotel in a restored 1920s Spanish building on State Street — bunk rooms to a rooftop penthouse, with an on-site coffee shop and Baja-inspired cocktail bar.

~0.6 mi to venues  ·  Member of Design Hotels

Other Great Options

La Playa Inn

A boutique mission-style inn with Spanish colonial architecture, free parking, and an open courtyard — within walking distance to the beach and an easy ride to the venues.

~1 block to the beach  ·  Free parking

The Milo

A hacienda-style beachfront hotel on West Beach with a pool, fire pits, complimentary bike cruisers, and an on-site café — steps from the sand.

Beachfront  ·  Pool & bikes included

Harbor View Inn

A 4-star oceanfront hotel sitting between West Beach and Stearns Wharf — every room has a private patio or balcony, with an ocean-view pool and jacuzzi on-site.

Oceanfront  ·  Ocean-view pool

Harbor House Inn

A family-owned collection of 17 studios one block from West Beach — complimentary beach gear, cruiser bikes, and fresh coffee from a local roaster included.

~1 block to West Beach  ·  Beach gear included

The Eagle Inn

A boutique B&B in the West Beach neighborhood with daily breakfast included — many rooms feature fireplaces, two-person tubs, and balconies, a short walk from the beach and Funk Zone.

~3 min walk to the beach  ·  Breakfast included
